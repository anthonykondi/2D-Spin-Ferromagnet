Goals:
- Numerically determine the probability of ending up in a metastable state for varying 
r = M/N (where M is the number of columns and N is the number of rows).
- Verify thermodynamic limit N, M -> inf for the case r = M/N = 1 fixed. Do this by getting
many samples for each N from {1, ..., N_max} and showing that as N increases, the sample mean 
of the fraction of metastable states of each kind gets closer (converges) to the theoretically 
expected probability.

Method:
- start with random lattice of spins
- update by randomly picking a spin and flip if it decreases energy
- stop once no more flips can be made
